## Conclusion

The concalls reveal a company in transformation:
- **Bull case:** AI momentum, margin expansion, large deal pipeline, Microsoft differentiation
- **Bear case:** Client concentration, international stagnation, domestic risks

Key insight: The market is underpricing the AI transformation and margin expansion story while overreacting to client-specific headwinds.
