# SONATA SOFTWARE Q1 FY26 Earnings Call
Date: July 30, 2025

## Press Release Summary

### Q1 FY26 Financial Results

| Metric | Q1 FY26 | QoQ | YoY |
|--------|---------|-----|-----|
| International Revenue | $81.8M | +0.6% QoQ | - |
| International Revenue (₹) | ₹699.9 cr | -0.3% | +1.8% |
| Domestic Revenue | ₹2,274.7 cr | +18.6% | +23.0% |
| Consolidated Revenue | ₹2,965.2 cr | +13.3% | +17.3% |
| EBITDA (Intl) | 16.6% | +0.1bps | - |
| PAT (Consolidated) | ₹109.3 cr | +1.7% | +3.5% |

### Key Developments in Q1

- **3 Large Deals Won:** 2 in BFSI, 1 in TMT
- **Mega Deal:** $73M AI-led Digital Modernization Deal with Leading US TMT Company
- **New Clients:** 7 new customers added
- **Healthcare + BFSI:** Now 32% of revenue (up from 13% 3 years ago)

### Management Commentary (CEO Samir Dhir)
"International IT Services Q1 marked steady progress with revenue growth of 0.6% QoQ. We secured three large deals - two in BFSI and one in TMT - underscoring our focus on large deals and execution strength. Our focused investments in Healthcare and BFSI have scaled from 13% to 32% of revenue over three years, validating our diversification strategy."

### Domestic Business (Sujit Mohanty)
"We gained new clients in cloud and data protection services. Our continued investments and partnerships with leading cloud providers position us well to grow revenue from cloud-managed services in the coming quarters."
