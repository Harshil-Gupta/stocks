# SONATA SOFTWARE Q3 FY26 Earnings Call
Date: February 6, 2026

## AI Summary

**Profitability:** Sonata improved EBITDA margin to 19.5% in Q3, up from 17.3% in the previous quarter, driven by operational efficiencies and AI adoption.

**Revenue Growth:** Consolidated Q3 revenue grew 45.4% quarter-on-quarter and 8.4% year-on-year; international services revenue grew 0.4% QoQ.

**AI Momentum:** AI-led business now forms 14% of the total order book, up from 10% last quarter, showing strong client demand and deeper integration.

**Large Deals:** Secured two large multiyear contracts in BFSI and payments, expected to support future growth.

**Client Headwinds:** Three out of the top ten clients faced headwinds impacting growth, especially in BFSI, TMT, and Retail; most of these impacts are now absorbed.

**Domestic Business Recovery:** Domestic segment saw 68.5% QoQ revenue growth; management expects to return to year-on-year growth by Q2 FY27.

**Utilization & Efficiency:** Utilization reached 90% (up from 87.3%), with offshore revenue mix improving and attrition stable at 11%.

**Outlook:** Management expects similar growth trajectory over next 1–2 quarters, with EBITDA margin guided in the 18–21% range going forward.

## Key Management Commentary

### Geographic & Segment Performance
- **Domestic Business:** Three-pillar strategy focusing on SMC segment (Microsoft channel), expansion into new ISVs (Oracle, IBM), and large system integration deals
- **BFSI & HLS:** Combined contribution rose to 31% of total revenue (up from 13% three years ago)
- **North America:** Now 70%+ of total revenue (up from 54% three years ago)

### Large Deal Wins
- Two multiyear deals closed: Fortune 500 digital wallet platform modernization, leading mortgage provider
- 40% of pipeline consists of large strategic opportunities

### AI & Modernization
- Launched AgentBridge (cloud-agnostic agentic AI platform)
- Partnering with IISc and Wharton for agentic AI research
- AI adoption improving internal productivity

## Red Flags from Concall

1. **Client Concentration Risk:** 3 of top 10 clients facing headwinds - ramp-downs in BFSI, TMT, Retail
2. **International Growth Stagnation:** Only 0.3% QoQ constant currency growth in international services
3. **Headcount Reduction:** Down from 6,649 to 6,404 - may indicate cost cutting vs growth
4. **Domestic Dependency:** Heavy reliance on domestic business recovery

---

## Bullish Triggers from Concall

1. **AI Momentum:** 14% of order book now AI-led (up from 10%)
2. **Margin Expansion:** EBITDA up 220 bps QoQ to 19.5%
3. **Large Deal Pipeline:** 40% of pipeline in large deals, two closed this quarter
4. **Utilization Improvement:** 90% utilization (record high)
5. **Microsoft Partnership:** Deepening Microsoft ecosystem play
